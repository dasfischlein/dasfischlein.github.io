Student Philosophy of Religion Conference

January 12th - 14th, 2007

Rutgers University

New Brunswick, New Jersey

 

This conference is intended to provide an informal setting in which Philosophy students can present papers on Philosophy of Religion to an audience of their peers, and receive comments from fellow students.

This conference is open to all who wish to attend, and there will not be any fees. If you are interested in presenting, please visit the Call For Papers section of this website for more information. If you would like to comment on a paper, or have questions or comments concerning the conference, please email the address to the right.
